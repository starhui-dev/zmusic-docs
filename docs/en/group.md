# Discord Community Rules {#discord-rules}

Rules for the [ZMusic Discord server](https://discord.gg/twQgJNufYn). By participating, you agree to follow these rules together with the [Discord Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines).

## Getting Help {#getting-help}

Before asking, please:

1. Search the [documentation](/en/guide/introduction) and [FAQ](/en/faq) — questions already answered there may not get a reply.
2. Make sure you are on the **latest version** — your issue may already be fixed.

When reporting an issue, include:

| What | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Version info | ZMusic version, server type and version (e.g. Paper 1.21.1) |
| Full log | The complete log from when the error occurred, shared via [mclo.gs](https://mclo.gs) — do not paste long logs into chat |
| Reproduction steps | What you did, what you expected, and what happened instead |

::: tip A good question looks like this
❌ "music not working, help"

✅ "Paper 1.21.1 + ZMusic 4.0.0-dev, running `/zmusic play <song>` shows xxx. Full log: https://mclo.gs/xxxxxx"
:::

Describe your problem and what you have already tried directly — no need to ask "anyone here?" first.

## Rules {#rules}

1. **Be respectful** — no harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or threats against anyone.
2. **No NSFW, gore, or illegal content** — in any form, including text, images, links, and voice.
3. **No spam** — no flooding, walls of repetitive text, or emoji/reaction spam.
4. **No unsolicited advertising** — server hosting, paid services, and similar promotions require prior approval from a moderator.
5. **No invite links** to other servers without permission.

## Enforcement {#enforcement}

Moderators act at their discretion. The usual escalation is **warning → timeout → ban**, but severe violations may lead to an immediate ban. If you believe a moderation action was a mistake, contact a moderator privately instead of arguing in public channels.
